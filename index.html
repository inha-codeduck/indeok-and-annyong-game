Get Started Play

Cooperative gameplay utilizing unique abilities

Indeok and Annyong's game is centered around thrilling cooperative gameplay. This game requires two players to control characters with different abilities and complete levels simultaneously. Indeok can pass through white liquid, while Annyong can pass through sky-blue liquid.

Enhancing problem-solving skills in the game through interaction and teamwork

Both characters must interact complementarily to solve each level's puzzles. The levels are filled with challenges that can only be completed by maximizing each character's abilities. Players progress through the game through interaction, teamwork, and strategic thinking. Through this process, players can enhance their cooperative spirit and problem-solving skills while enjoying the game.
